STEPS TO GROW CARROT

Step 1 : Plant your carrots in Spring (February - March) or in Fall (October - November)
Step 2 : Carrot Plants need direct sunlight
Step 3 :Plant Carrots in Soft, Permeable Loose Soil
Step 4 :Plant seeds 2 inches away from each other
Step 5 :Use a container that is 18 inches deep
Step 6 :Water carrots regularly
Step 7 :Protect carrots from pests and diseases
Step 8 :Harvest carrots and enjoy fresh carrots
