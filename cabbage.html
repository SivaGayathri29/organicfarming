STEPS TO GROW CABBAGE

Step1: Start seeds indoors 4 to 6 weeks before the last frost in spring.
Step2: Place cabbage transplants in the garden when they are 3 to 4 inches (7-10cm) tall as early as 3 to 4 weeks before the last frost in spring.
Step3: Direct sow seed outdoors when the soil can be worked in spring.
Step4: In mild-winter regions, start seed in late summer for a winter or spring harvest.
